Story

A Kingdom Swallowed by Darkness

For centuries, the Kingdom of Solara thrived beneath the light of the Eternal Sun. But when the Black Eclipse appeared, daylight vanished, cities crumbled, and noble knights became cursed guardians of a forgotten age.

You are the last bearer of the Moonfire Blade, the only weapon capable of breaking the curse. Travel across ruined castles, haunted forests, and forgotten battlefields as you uncover the truth behind the eclipse and challenge the ancient dragon guarding the stolen sun.

Every step brings you closer to the final battle. Every choice shapes the fate of the world.
